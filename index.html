Module 03

Train Games

One word. One sentence. No limits. Build together.

Player 1 starts with a word. Player 2 adds a reason. Player 3 adds detail. Player 4 makes it a request. No reference. No rules. Just speak.

💡 Example Flow

"Hotel" → "Need hotel room" → "Need hotel room because tired" → "Could you help me find a hotel room because I'm exhausted?"

1 Starter — One word or short phrase

Hotel... tired...

2 Builder — Add a reason

Hotel... tired... because my flight was delayed.

3 Expander — Add detail or emotion

Hotel... tired... because my flight was delayed... and I really need somewhere to rest.

4 Closer — Make it a complete request

Hotel... tired... because my flight was delayed... and I really need somewhere to rest... Could you please help me find a room?
