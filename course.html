Module 2

Build Your Scenarios

Learn essential phrases for real-world situations

🎫 Airport Check-in

Agent: Good morning! May I see your passport and booking confirmation?

You: Here you go. I'd also like to request an aisle seat, please.

Agent: Let me check... Yes, we can do that. Are you checking any bags?

You: Just this one suitcase.

💡 Tip: Always confirm your seat preference early!

🛫 Flight Delay

You: Excuse me, I heard our flight is delayed. What's the new departure time?

Agent: Yes, we're looking at a 2-hour delay due to weather.

You: I have a connecting flight in London. Will I miss it?

Agent: Let me rebook you. We'll provide meal vouchers for the wait.

💡 Tip: Ask about compensation for long delays!

🏨 Hotel Upgrade

You: Hi, I'm celebrating my anniversary. Do you have any complimentary upgrades available?

Receptionist: Let me check... We do have a suite with a city view.

You: That sounds perfect! Is breakfast included?

Receptionist: Yes, all suite guests get complimentary breakfast.

💡 Tip: Special occasions often qualify for free upgrades!

🎫 Gate Change

Announcement: Attention passengers, Flight BA123 to London is now boarding at Gate 42.

You: Excuse me, where is Gate 42?

Staff: It's in Terminal B. Take the express train.

You: How long does the train take?

Staff: About 5 minutes.

💡 Tip: Always double-check your gate before boarding!

🧳 Lost Luggage

You: My luggage didn't arrive. What should I do?

Agent: I'm sorry to hear that. Let me file a report. Do you have your baggage claim tag?

You: Yes, here it is. My bag is black with a red ribbon.

Agent: We'll deliver it to your hotel once we find it.

💡 Tip: Always keep your baggage claim tag safe!

✈️ Business Lounge

You: Hi, I have a Priority Pass membership. Can I access the lounge?

Staff: Absolutely! May I see your boarding pass and membership card?

You: Here you go. How long can I stay?

Staff: Up to 3 hours before your flight. Enjoy our amenities!

💡 Tip: Lounge access often includes free food and WiFi!
